About EarnKart

Our Mission

EarnKart is on a mission to democratize access to employment opportunities by connecting local workers with immediate job opportunities. We believe everyone deserves a fair chance to earn a livelihood with dignity.

Founded in 2025, EarnKart has helped thousands of workers find temporary and permanent jobs while assisting employers in finding reliable help for their needs.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no one is unemployed due to lack of access to job opportunities. Through technology, we aim to bridge the gap between job seekers and providers in local communities.

Our platform is designed specifically for rural and semi-urban areas where traditional job portals often fail to reach.

How It Works

For Job Seekers: Browse available gigs in your area, apply with one click, and start earning immediately. No complicated resumes needed. EarnKart verifies all job postings to ensure safety and reliability.

For Employers: Post your job requirements, connect with verified local workers, and get your work done efficiently. Our platform makes hiring simple, quick, and cost-effective.

Safety & Trust

At EarnKart, we prioritize the safety and security of both job seekers and employers. Our platform includes:

  • User verification through phone and ID checks
  • Secure messaging system for communication
  • Rating and review system for accountability
  • 24/7 customer support for any issues
  • Clear guidelines for fair pay and working conditions
