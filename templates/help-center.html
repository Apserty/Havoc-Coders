Popular Questions

How do I create an account on EarnKart?
Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your details (name, email, phone number), and verify your phone number. Once verified, you can start browsing jobs or posting work immediately.
How are payments handled on EarnKart?
Payments can be handled in two ways: 1) Direct cash payment between worker and employer, or 2) Through EarnKart's secure payment system. We recommend discussing payment methods with the other party before starting work. Always get payment confirmation in the app.
Is there any fee for using EarnKart?
EarnKart is completely free for workers to find jobs. For employers posting jobs, there's a small 5% service fee on the total job value, which helps us maintain and improve the platform.
How do I report a safety concern?
If you encounter any safety concerns, please immediately contact our support team through the "Contact Support" page. You can also use the emergency button in the app to report urgent safety issues. We take all safety reports seriously and respond within 24 hours.
