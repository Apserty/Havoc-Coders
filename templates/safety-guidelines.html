Safety First

Your safety is our top priority. Follow these guidelines to ensure a secure experience on EarnKart

IMPORTANT: Emergency Contacts

If you feel unsafe or encounter an emergency situation:

  • Police: 100
  • Women's Helpline: 1091
  • EarnKart Emergency: +91 1800-123-4567
  • Local Emergency Services: 112

Always trust your instincts. If something feels wrong, remove yourself from the situation immediately.

Safety Tips for Everyone

1

Verify Profiles

Always check user ratings and verification badges before accepting work or hiring.

2

Meet in Public

For first meetings, choose public places like cafes or community centers.

3

Inform Someone

Always let a friend or family member know where you're going and who you're meeting.

4

Use In-App Communication

Keep all communication within the EarnKart app for your safety and records.

5

Check Work Environment

Before starting work, ensure the environment is safe and appropriate for the job.

6

Trust Your Instincts

If something feels wrong or too good to be true, it probably is. Report suspicious activity.

Safety Guidelines for Workers

  • Research Employers: Check employer ratings and reviews before accepting jobs
  • Clarify Job Details: Get clear information about work hours, location, and requirements
  • Discuss Payment: Agree on payment terms and method before starting work
  • Bring Essentials: Carry water, snacks, and necessary safety equipment
  • Document Work: Take photos of completed work for your records
  • Report Issues: Immediately report any unsafe conditions or inappropriate behavior

Safety Guidelines for Employers

  • Verify Workers: Check worker profiles, ratings, and verification status
  • Provide Clear Instructions: Give detailed job descriptions and safety guidelines
  • Ensure Safe Workspace: Maintain a safe working environment with necessary equipment
  • Respect Boundaries: Maintain professional behavior and respect personal space
  • Timely Payments: Pay workers promptly as agreed
  • Provide Breaks: Ensure workers get adequate breaks and access to water
